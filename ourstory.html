Our Story

We first met online in 2018 via a dating app called Coffee Meets Bagel. Although the restaurant wasn't that great, we liked each other. Over the next few months we continued to date and form a strong bond. Danielle's risk taker attitude entrigued Michael and his wit kept her wanting to know more. In February 2020, during one of their trips, Michael proposed in San Diego at the Sunset Cliffs and Danielle said yes! Little did we know that the world would shut down the next month due to COVID. As things seemed to go up and down, we closed on a house and tried to have some sense of normalcy, but with the unforseen ahead, we decided to elope, just the two of us, in Colorado and then roadtrip around the state for a week. We enjoyed this time and it allowed us to enter our marriage with adventure and quality time, but now it's time for everyone to celebrate with us at our traditional ceremony!
